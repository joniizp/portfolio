Project #1: asleep

This flarf poem was built off of a text i sent my boyfriend in the morning. "I fell back asleep" i texted after letting him know i overslept in the morning. Google search results of this quote led me to a forum post about grammar. The final poem i created questions our reality vs. the dream world.

Project #2: think of yooko

Experimenting with Zoom's green screen feature, i created a music video for NEW YORK's "Think of You". This piece is titled "think of yooko" in honor of my baby (cat). Truly, i think of him all day.

Project #3: you deserve this

Inspired by own troubles balancing school, work, and a social life, this twine story takes you through an evening of social self care. You will come across oversized fashion pieces, flickr imagery from the 2000s, and maybe even Addison Rae.

Project #4: cakes

I've been baking for years and only recently decided to get creative. At the beginning of the year, i could not find a cake artist to make me a birthday cake. Therefore, i took matters into my own hands. It has become an expensive but very fulfilling hobby. (:
